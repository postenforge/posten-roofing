# Posten Roofing — Design Brainstorm

## Three Approaches

### 1. Craftsman Industrial
A raw, honest aesthetic inspired by construction materials — exposed textures, steel beams, concrete surfaces. Dark and moody with copper/amber highlights.
**Probability:** 0.03

### 2. Wisconsin Homestead
Warm, grounded, and approachable — like a well-built Wisconsin home. Natural tones, strong horizontal lines (like rooflines), generous whitespace, and a feeling of shelter and protection.
**Probability:** 0.07

### 3. Precision Documentation
Clinical and trust-forward — inspired by inspection reports and photographic evidence. Clean grids, sharp contrast, documentary-style imagery. The site itself feels like proof.
**Probability:** 0.04

---

## Chosen Approach: Wisconsin Homestead

### Design Movement
Midwest Modern — a blend of mid-century warmth with contemporary construction confidence. Think: the honest beauty of a well-framed house, not a Silicon Valley startup.

### Core Principles
1. **Shelter**: Every section should feel like it's protecting the content beneath it — strong horizontal rooflines, angled dividers, and layered depth
2. **Warmth Over Flash**: Amber/gold tones that connect to the family's Above All Roofing heritage, grounded in charcoal and cream
3. **Straight Talk**: The site communicates like a knowledgeable neighbor — clear, honest, no pressure. No forced tagline or branded hook — just direct communication about what we do and how we help.
4. **Directness**: Copy is short, specific, and action-oriented. No marketing fluff.

### Color Philosophy
The palette draws from the materials of roofing itself — dark shingle charcoal as the foundation, warm amber/copper as the accent (like sunset light on a new roof), and clean cream/white as the sky. This connects emotionally to the Dad's Above All Roofing brand (navy + gold sunset) while establishing Posten Roofing's own identity.

- **Foundation**: Deep charcoal `#1C1F26` — like architectural shingles
- **Sky**: Warm cream `#FAF7F2` — clean, not sterile
- **Accent**: Amber copper `#C8702A` — the signature brand color, warm and ownable
- **Trust**: Deep navy `#1E3A5F` — for headings and authority
- **Alert**: Warm red `#B8372B` — for urgency (leaks, storm damage)

### Layout Paradigm
**Roofline Architecture** — sections are separated by angled dividers that mimic the pitch of a roof. Content flows in asymmetric two-column layouts where imagery sits at the "eave" and text sits under the "ridge." The page feels like you're walking through a well-built house, room by room.

### Signature Elements
1. **Angled Section Dividers**: Every major section transition uses a subtle 3-5° angle cut, mimicking roof pitch
2. **Photo Evidence Cards**: Key content is presented in "inspection report" style cards with a photo, caption, and action
3. **The Gold Underline**: Important headings get a short amber underline — a nod to the Dad's site's gold accent borders

### Interaction Philosophy
Interactions should feel **solid and deliberate** — like setting a nail, not like swiping a phone. Buttons have weight (slight press animation), forms feel structured (clear fields, progress indication), and hover states reveal additional information rather than just changing color.

### Animation
- Page sections fade-and-rise on scroll (subtle, 200ms, ease-out)
- Buttons scale down 2% on press with 120ms transition
- The Roof Triage Tool options slide in with 40ms stagger
- Navigation transitions are instant — no page-level animations
- Mobile sticky CTAs slide up from bottom on first scroll
- Reduced motion: all animations disabled

### Typography System
- **Display/H1**: "DM Serif Display" — authoritative, warm serif with character
- **Headings/H2-H4**: "Inter" weight 700 — clean and modern
- **Body**: "Inter" weight 400 — highly readable
- **Accent/Labels**: "Inter" weight 600 uppercase tracking-wide — for badges and labels

### Brand Essence
Posten Roofing is honest roof help for Wisconsin homeowners who want straight answers — built on a family roofing background, delivered with directness and care. No branded hook or tagline — the work speaks for itself.
**Personality**: Grounded, Trustworthy, Direct

### Brand Voice
Headlines sound like a neighbor who knows roofing giving you straight talk. CTAs are specific actions, never vague. Microcopy acknowledges homeowner anxiety and meets it with clarity.
- Example headline: "Storm hit last night? Here's what to check before you call anyone."
- Example CTA: "Check my roof" / "Get a straight answer"

### Wordmark & Logo
A bold "P" letterform with an integrated roofline peak — the ridge of the P's bowl becomes a clean roof angle. Set in a custom-weight geometric sans. The full wordmark reads "POSTEN ROOFING" in tracked uppercase with the P-mark preceding it.

### Signature Brand Color
**Amber Copper `#C8702A`** — warm, ownable, visible against both dark and light backgrounds, connects to the family's gold/sunset branding heritage without copying it.
